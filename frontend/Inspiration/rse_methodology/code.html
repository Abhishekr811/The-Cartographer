Methodology

Our approach to structuring knowledge relies on transparent, verifiable processes. We prioritize clarity over complexity, ensuring every connection can be traced back to its source.

How claims are extracted

Source documents are parsed into fundamental assertions. We isolate distinct statements of fact or opinion, removing rhetorical flourish to expose the core argument.

How relationships are identified

Extracted claims are then mapped against existing knowledge. We look for corroboration, contradiction, or thematic alignment, weaving individual points into a broader structural narrative.

How confidence is determined

Every connection is assigned a qualitative weight based on source provenance and historical reliability. This is not an absolute truth score, but a measure of structural integrity within the graph.
